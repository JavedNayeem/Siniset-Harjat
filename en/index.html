    Our Humble Beginnings

The blue colour is Siniset Harjat the wheel that motivates and drives it because blue colour represent freedom, fostering peace and security. Our services

Your cleaning is our Responsibility

Home cleaning

We clean your home as you direct and prefer. All corners and angles of your house will be attended to. Our employee will ensure your house shine like the flashlight.

Windows cleaning

Windows set the stage for a home's interior design, mood and appearance, thus being tidy and shining is key to these factors to uphold. Our professionals will do all that for you and your house or office.

Moving Cleanliness

Are intending to change your home to another? Do you worry about cleaning of your mess? Leave this stress for Siniset Harjat’s professionals. All you can do is just focus on packing your belongings and leave the dirt and dust for us.

Office Space cleaning

A clean workplace is important, and it will always benefit employers to make sure that their premises are regularly cleaned. Tidy and clean office environment creates positive impression and professional image. We tell you this because we know how to handle this diligent service.
